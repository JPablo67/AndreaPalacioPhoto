About

The Eye Behind the Lens

I'm Andrea Palacio — a commercial and editorial photographer based between Monterrey and Houston. I specialize in creating elevated visual narratives that help brands tell their story with intention and artistry.

With a background rooted in editorial fashion and a passion for luxury aesthetics, I bring a director's eye to every project — whether it's a five-star hotel, a chef-driven restaurant, or a bold brand campaign.

My dual-market presence in Mexico and the United States gives me a versatile, global perspective that resonates with international brands seeking authentic visual storytelling.
